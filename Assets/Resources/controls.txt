Controls:

-WASD or arrow keys to move around

-Hold shift to move around quicker

-Hold right mouse button to look around

-Space to ascend

-Left Ctrl to descend

-Lock Camera: locks the camera in place and follows the dancers, press it again to unlock camera

-Reset: reset the position of the dancers and the camera

-Use the slider to adjust the speed

-Press pause to stop the step, press play to continue
